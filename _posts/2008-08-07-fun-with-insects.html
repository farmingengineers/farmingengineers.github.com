--- layout: post title: Fun with Insects tags: - Mrs. B. status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1494310' --- The cucumber beetles are descending in hordes.  Every time I go out to harvest, thousands of them fly off the cucumber plants & I seem to always bring a few in the house. Two fat tomato worms were dispatched to the chickens yesterday.  Makes for happy chickens!
